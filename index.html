About Me

Senior Data Analytics Engineer & Consultant

I'm a Senior Data Analytics Engineer & Consultant based in Manchester, specialising in designing and delivering world-class Power BI solutions for enterprise clients. My work spans Energy, Insurance, Banking, and Financial Services — sectors where the quality of your data and the clarity of your reporting directly impacts millions of pounds of decisions.

My core expertise is the full Microsoft analytics stack — Power BI (advanced DAX, paginated reports, embedded analytics, row-level security), Microsoft Fabric (Lakehouse, Warehouse, Dataflows Gen2, semantic models), and the wider Azure cloud platform (Synapse Analytics, Databricks, Data Lake, Data Factory). I architect scalable data platforms from the ground up and deliver reporting that executives actually use.

I'm equally passionate about automation and the Power Platform — using Power Automate and Power Apps to eliminate manual processes, connect systems, and put data-driven tools directly in the hands of the people who need them. If it's a Microsoft product, I know how to make it work harder for your business.
